20 March 2020

Hello from one of Good-Loop's new satellite offices!

As we all bunker down at our kitchen tables fear not, Good-Loop is still here to bring you a little bit of Good News from the world of advertising.

However, this edition is slightly different as it also includes a look at the real-world impact of brands doing good in the face of COVID-19 as well as tips for staying sane whilst you're WFH and also a Good-Loop playlist (the name itself is worth reading on for).

Starting off with some unusual activity in our new Croydon satellite office - An unexpected guest hadn't got the WFH memo... (This is not Julia's cat)
Over on the Good-Loop LinkedIn, you can also make a donation to the World Health Organization with just a few seconds of your time. Every view in the next 48 hours will unlock a £0.05 donation and together we can raise £5,000 to fight COVID-19.
We would love to hear how you're all keeping yourselves entertained at the moment. Have you got any tips on how to make WFH a little bit more fun?

Stay safe,
The Good-Loop Team.
Brands are becoming forces for good in these uncertain times by providing moments of joy and solidarity to those who need it most.

It was amazing to wake up the news this week that Pret would be giving all NHS workers a free hot drink, and as the day went on more brands also started shouting their thanks to those fighting on the frontline.

McDonald's, Dominos, and even Gary Neville's Hotel Football are all offering freebies to NHS workers. But we rarely get to see the real-world impact they have in action. We're all likely to know someone now who absolutely deserves a free coffee to start their day so get spreading the good news.
